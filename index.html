Ingredients

  • Cornbread
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Sage
  • Poultry Seasoning

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Stir together all ingredients
  3. Transfer to greased dish
  4. Bake for 30 minutes until brown on top
